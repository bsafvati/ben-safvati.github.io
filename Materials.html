Physics Materials

This page will host a sample of the work that I have done throughout my academic career. This includes reports and presentations for classes, university requirements, and a journal club within my current lab. Many of these projects reference research in quantum materials and experiments involving STM, but there is some variety into other areas of condensed matter physics and quantum information science. If any of this material is of interest to you, I would be happy to hear your comments or questions. Also please note that all of these slides were meant to accompany an oral presentation, but I hope that looking through them will help introduce new science material and provide context for the main results.

Any published papers from my research in experimental physics can be found at my google scholar page, which you can reach by clicking the university building icon on the bottom of the webpage. If you don't have reading access for any of these papers, please send me an email and I will try to help out.
